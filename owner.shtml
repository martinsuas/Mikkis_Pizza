About Us

The Owner

Hi! I'm Mikki Kanagawa!

I grew up in Japan, but have always loved American cuisine! My favorite one is of course, pizza! Since I was little, I always dreamed of owning my own little pizzeria. Now that I have successfully migrated to America, I can finally make my dream come true (since nobody eats pizza in Japan)!

Our Philosophy

Pizza is not just food. It's an art. At Mikki's Pizzeria, we believe art should be done only with the freshest, rawest materials. That's why we only use fresh, organic ingredients in all of our pizzas. All our dough is homemade, and you'll find that our oven-cooked pizzas are the crunchiest, tastiest, and most delicious in the market!

But don't take our word for it! Come try it yourself!
